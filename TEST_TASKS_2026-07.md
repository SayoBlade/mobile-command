# Playtest task list — ~1hr, 2 testers (2026-07)

**Method: observational usability test.** Give the tester the GOAL, never the steps.
Say *"buy yourself some armour"* — never *"tap Equipment, then…"*. If they get stuck,
wait ~20s before helping, and note WHERE they stuck. Every "how do I…?" is a finding.

**Legend:** `[ ]` planned · `[x]` covered in this build · `[~]` partially/needs prompt
Add tasks freely; tick the ones you actually run.

---

## A. Character creation & identity
- [ ] "Make your character." (full char-gen: species, class, abilities, equipment)
- [ ] "Give your character a face." (portrait generation)
- [ ] "Your character has a story — **write a short bio for them.**" (bio editor + save)
- [ ] Mid/late session: "You got a scar from that pit trap — **add a note about it to your bio.**" (re-open bio, edit, save)
- [ ] "Set your character's name / rename them." (rename flow)
- [ ] "What colour do you want to be?" (DM triggers colour pick → tester chooses)

## B. Inn — shopping & loot (the "Use" verb, no how-to)
- [ ] "There's a pile of loot on the floor — **take it.**" (Use → pile)
- [ ] "Buy yourself some gear from the shopkeeper." (Use → merchant, purchase)
- [ ] "Buy the anklets that let you feel vibrations." (the tremorsense item → equip)
- [ ] "Write down something the quest-giver told you." (party journal note)

## C. Overworld travel (Party Mode)
- [ ] "You're travelling together — **get in formation.**" (Form up → arrange the 3×3)
- [ ] "Put your pet/familiar somewhere in the line too." (multi-token: move BOTH owned tokens)
- [ ] "Lead the party to the cave." (travel pad drives the group token)
- [ ] "Someone should scout ahead." (release a scout — a pet is ideal)
- [ ] "Regroup." (combine the scout back)
- [ ] "Do you notice anything on the road?" (group Perception/Stealth check)

## D. Cave — traps, combat, senses
- [ ] "Head into the cave." (cross into the cave scene → disperse)
- [ ] Pit trap at the mouth fires on movement (MATT tile) — do they react?
- [ ] Vision split: darkvision tester sees greyscale terrain; tremorsense tester sees
      only creature blips in the dark. Ask each *"what can you see?"*
- [ ] "Something's here — **fight it.**" (combat: attack, target, damage)
- [ ] "Summon your familiar / creature to help." (summon flow → second owned token)
- [ ] "You're hurt — **do something about it.**" (potion / spell / short rest w/ hit dice)
- [ ] Let the pet drop to 0 HP → death-save screen appears (never live-tested)
- [ ] A condition lands (prone/frightened) → **"tap it to see what it does."** (cond tap-to-open)

## E. Investigation → secret door → reward
- [ ] "The wall looks odd — **check it.**" (Investigation; DM roll-request via D20 dock)
- [ ] Secret door revealed → "**open it.**" (Use → door)
- [ ] "There's treasure — **take it.**" (Use → pile)
- [ ] Victory music plays (DM playlist) — confirm it reaches the phones/TV.

## F. Free observation (whole session)
- [ ] Any moment a tester says *"how do I…"* — log the verbatim quote + what they expected.
- [ ] Anything they tried that DIDN'T work (dead taps, wrong mental model).
- [ ] Anything they liked out loud.
