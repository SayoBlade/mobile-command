## B. New bugs (proposed batch-2, in priority order)

1. **Cancelled advancement can't be resumed** — Tomo cancelled a wizard mid-flow:
   "ברגע שביטלתי את ה-advancements הוא לא מציע את זה מחדש" [23:33]. The class item
   sits on the sheet with its steps unapplied and no way to re-trigger → the "empty
   character sheet" the DM had to rebuild [23:57]. Background-granted skills also
   suspected missing [34:12]. **Fix:** detect incomplete advancements on the char-gen
   workspace and offer "Resume setup for <item>" (AdvancementManager re-run).
2. **Char-gen should end in a long rest** (DM's own diagnosis): fresh/rebuilt PCs had
   0 spell slots — "you have no available first level" [49:16], solved live by a long
   rest [Tomo 55:11]. **Fix:** `#finishCharGen` runs a silent long rest (a brand-new
   PC has nothing to lose), replacing the narrower slot top-up path.
3. **Shield reaction error:** "You must provide an embedded Document instance"
   [Tomo 37:07] on casting Shield as a reaction (worked later [52:07]). **TRACED
   (2026-07-05):** the string is Foundry core's PlaceableObject constructor
   (foundry.mjs:54318) — something built a CANVAS object on the canvas-less phone
   during the player-side cast, almost certainly an animation module
   (AutoAnimations/Sequencer) firing on the No-Canvas client (same family as the
   action-pack-enhanced errors). Not our pipeline. Mitigation → onboarding:
   disable Sequencer effects client-side on phones (Sequencer has a per-client
   "enable effects" setting); consider not installing combat-HUD/animation
   helpers for phone users at all.
4. **Magic Missile can't stack darts on one target** — target picker refuses picking
   the same enemy twice [Tomo 50:37]. Per-dart allocation needs repeats.
5. **AC breakdown card omits active effects** — AC showed 15 (correct) but OUR
   breakdown listed only the base calc; Mage Armor invisible [Tomo 55:38, DM 55:46].
   (DM attributed it to Foundry live; the breakdown card is ours.)
6. **Psychic Blade (Soulknife)** — DM had to intervene on the ATTACK [45:08] and it
   stayed flaky [47:04 "יש בו משהו לא בסדר"]. Sneak attack likely fine (DM). **Needs
   a live repro with the console open before touching the executor.**
7. **Item Piles merchant: purchases give no visible feedback** ("לא נראה שזה קנה לך…
   אני לא רואה שקיבלת" [30:33–31:32]) **and the window is still oversized on phones**
   [30:48] even with the portrait hidden (v0.1.78). Phone-fit round 2 + a buy-toast.
8. **Advancement dialog phone-fit** — the lifted wizard "covers half the phone", the
   button underneath unreachable, can't shrink/move [Tomo 25:52–26:08]. Known backlog
   (dense ~563px dialog); the playtest confirms it's a top irritant.
9. **Unexplained one-offs to watch** (no repro yet): "cannot read properties of null"
   at the inn door [Tomo 38:29]; mid-fight turn confusion [DM 47:50]; cone template
   couldn't catch both targets [DM 49:57].
